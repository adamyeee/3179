Global Population Overview

The global population exceeds 8 billion

Asia has the largest population by far

Africa and Europe are in the middle

NA and Oceania have smaller populations

This data does not consider mortality rates

China and India have the highest birth rates

US and Germany are growing rapidly due to migrants

Monaco and Singapore have high densities due to their limited land area and large populations

Canada and Australia have low population densities due to vast land areas and relatively smaller populations

Median age and fertility rate are strongly correlated

Median age is a reliable predictor of future population growth

Australia has a relatively low fertility rate and a slower population growth
